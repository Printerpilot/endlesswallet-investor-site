Market Opportunity

Capitalizing on a New Opportunity

Traditional credit infrastructure excludes millions while peer-to-peer lending remains fragmented and limited. We're building the comprehensive solution the market needs.

Market Gaps

  • 80 million Americans have poor or limited credit history
  • Traditional P2P platforms focus only on high-credit borrowers
  • No secondary market for loan notes limits liquidity
  • Small businesses lack tools to finance customers
  • Lack of solutions in the market for seamlessly extending credit to family
  • Unbanked populations globally have no credit access
  • FICO scores often misrepresent actual creditworthiness

Our Solution

  • Alternative credit scoring (FAP system) for holistic assessment
  • Comprehensive loan structuring with flexible terms
  • Note marketplace enabling loan trading and liquidity
  • Tools for businesses to extend credit directly
  • Multi-currency and crypto support for global access
  • Social verification and relationship-based lending

Comprehensive Lending Infrastructure

Beyond simple loan matching—we're building end-to-end infrastructure for the future of peer-to-peer finance.

Alternative Credit Assessment

Financial Achievement Points (FAP) system evaluates creditworthiness beyond FICO—incorporating verified identity, social connections, net worth composition, and repayment history for holistic risk assessment.

Flexible Loan Structuring

Customizable terms including APR, repayment schedules, collateral requirements, governing law, and currency. Borrowers create detailed petitions; lenders negotiate directly.

Secondary Note Marketplace

Trade loan notes like securities. Sell full positions, partial stakes, or package multiple loans together. Creates liquidity in traditionally illiquid asset class.

Revolving Credit Lines

Extend credit lines to verified connections. Set limits, rates, and terms. Enables businesses to finance customers and families to support each other financially.

Multi-Currency & Crypto Support

Lend and borrow in USD, EUR, GBP, plus USDC, USDT, BTC, and ETH. Removes geographic barriers and serves global, unbanked populations.

Automated Contract Generation

E-signature integration via HelloSign. Customizable legal templates. Jurisdiction selection. Creates enforceable agreements with automated tracking and notifications.

Important Note on Risk

Peer-to-peer lending involves substantial risk of loss. The platform is currently in sandbox/pre-launch stage with no operational history. Investors should only invest capital they can afford to lose and should diversify across multiple loans to manage risk once the platform launches.

Built for Scale and Compliance

Enterprise-grade infrastructure designed for regulatory compliance, security, and global scalability.

Security & Compliance

Bank-level encryption (AES-256), PCI compliance via Stripe, KYC/AML verification, and SOC 2 infrastructure. Designed with regulatory requirements at the core.

Payment Infrastructure

Integrated with Stripe for ACH transfers and Plaid for bank account verification. Real-time payment tracking and automated repayment processing.

Scalable Architecture

Sub-100ms API response times. Real-time marketplace pricing. Automated contract generation. Built to handle institutional-scale transaction volumes.
